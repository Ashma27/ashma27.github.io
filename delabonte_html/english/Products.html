OUR PRODUCTS

Whether you need a single chisel table or more, Daniel Labonté Industries Inc. offers a variety of standard size tables. However, our strength is custom- manufacturing according to the needs of the customer, regardless of the size, heights or capabilities required.

HYDRAULIC SCISSOR LIFT TABLES

The hydraulic scissor lift tables are Industries Daniel Labonté’s most coveted products. Solid, simple and available in several sizes, they are used for load handling and allow for improved ergonomics in factories.

IDL offers tables in various sizes and capacities ranging from 1,000 lbs. to 15,000 lbs. Moreover, they can be tailored and adapted to your needs.

HYDRAULIC DOUBLE SCISSOR LIFT TABLES

The hydraulic double scissor lift tables consist of two sets of superposed scissors, which can reach higher than the single scissor lift tables. This model can be used as a working platform for stacking pallets or as a work table.

IDL offers tables in various sizes and capacities ranging from 1000 lbs. to 6000 lbs. Moreover, they can be tailored and adapted to your needs.

MORE DETAILS

TILTING TABLES

The tilt table is used to incline the lift table to facilitate the handling and reduce injuries.

The tilt can be customised to suit individual needs and wishes of the customer.
