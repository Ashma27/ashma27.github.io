OUR PRODUCTS

Whether you need a single chisel table or more, Daniel Labonté Industries Inc. offers a variety of standard size tables. However, our strength is custom- manufacturing according to the needs of the customer, regardless of the size, heights or capabilities required.

Hydraulic scissor lift tables

The hydraulic scissor lift tables are Industries Daniel Labonté’s most coveted products. Solid, simple and available in several sizes, they are used for load handling and allow for improved ergonomics in factories.

IDL offers tables in various sizes and capacities ranging from 1,000 lbs. to 15,000 lbs. Moreover, they can be tailored and adapted to your needs.

OUR TABLES INCLUDE

  • 110V single action hydraulic unit under the table
  • 3/16" thick platform
  • Manual controls
  • Non-lubricated pivotal point
  • Precision ground shaft
  • Single action cylinder
  • Velocity fuse
  • Blue color

OPTIONS AVAILABLE

  • Special sizes upon request
  • Wheel Kit (for 4000 lbs. and under)
  • 6-inch wheels
  • Secure tape switch
  • Safety curtain
  • Ball bearing-mounted plate
  • Automat with photocell
  • Lifting eyelets
  • 600V service box
  • Handling sash
  • Checkered plate platform
  • Parapet
  • Boarding ramp
  • Table tilt
  • Double action
  • Foot pedal
  • Circular or rectangular rotating platform
  • Stop limiter
  • Roller conveyor
  • Lubricatable pivotal points

HYDRAULIC UNIT OPTIONS

  • 12V/DC
  • 220V
  • 600V
  • Hydropneumatic units
