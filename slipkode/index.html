Our potential user

Every small or medium sized business owner who still runs his business sales record over a paper and a pen is our ideal customer.

Using this solution will enable them to keep track of their business on the go through our dashboard and customised mobile application. The user can even get all his financial and accounting statements from our dashboard which helps him to deposit VAT and other taxes with the government and keep his business running.

The challenging part was to implement both the layouts in a single APK that would automatically fetch the UI based on the device it is opened from.
