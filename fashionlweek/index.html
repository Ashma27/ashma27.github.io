Fashion Show Monde World Mundo

Beyond a fashion show, the Cosmopolitan Fashion Week (Fashion Show World/FSMW) is a contemporary and social movement that fosters networking and incorporates design, music, conferences, and an exhibition of haute couture and eccentric garments, not to mention the nightlife with International DJs.

The Cosmopolitan Fashion Week (FSMW) aims to show a better face of the world through a cosmopolitan fashion show, and share a message of peace, union and living together regardless of differences.
