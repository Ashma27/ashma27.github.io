In addition, when you visit our website, we will record your computer IP address and/or unique mobile device ID, and may tie it to your specific account. Under ordinary circumstances, an IP address recorded in isolation is not PII under the standard definition of the term. However, it may become PII when it is combined with other information, as in this case.

What is Non Personal Information?

Non-Personal Information is information that does not identify you, and may include statistical information concerning the pages on our website that users visit most frequently. General Information of this nature is collected through a small piece of computer code called a “cookie,” and includes your computer IP address, unique mobile device identifier, browser type, ISP or carrier name, and the URL of the last webpage you visited before visiting our website.
